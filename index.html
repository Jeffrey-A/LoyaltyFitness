About Loyalty Fitness 365

At Loyalty Fitness 365, we are establishing a bond between you and your fitness goals. We are creating a customized workout experience through technology and customer service that will benefit the Bronx community and help you turn your fitness dreams into reality.

This new fitness facility will bring many amenities not currently offered in the South Bronx, such as a juice bar, dance, yoga, pilates, and kickboxing classes, physical therapy, sauna, steam room, and a full size basketball court all in a 3-floor building.

This concept is important for the Mott Haven neighborhood because Brookfield is bringing 1300 new multifamily units and according to our market research, the only fitness centers available are crossfit and boxing gyms. Thus, we want to give residents a different option to enhance their experience and engagement in their community.
